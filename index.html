Our Menu

BRIYANI

➢Sindhi Biryani: Spicy, fragrant, and popular in Pakistan.

➢Hyderabadi Biryani: Goat meat, coconut, and saffron are key ingredients.

➢Malabar Biryani: Unique to Kerala, made with Khyma rice and ghee.

➢Calcutta/Kolkata Biryani: Lighter spice-wise, often includes potatoes and eggs

MEALS

➢Vegetarian: Dishes like paneer tikka masala (paneer in tomato-based gravy) or chana masala (chickpeas in spicy sauce).

➢Non-Vegetarian: Chicken curry, lamb korma, or fish curry.

FAST FOODS
